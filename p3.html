Conversational agent for Railway enquiry

Indian railway passengers can perform tasks like Book a ticket, Find trains etc., through Google assistant(Hindi) on mobile phone or kiosk on the platform instead of face-to-face enquiry.

"I think this is a good utility to convert an otherwise tedious task into a more easy, everyday activity, so yes I do like the idea of such an agent"

Abhishek

Passenger since 2012

Railways holds a complex set of information and processes which provides a confusion regarding planning a journey or about a booked journey. To assist passengers with their questions, Indian railways has setup a face-to-face enquiry system on the stations and also have different websites and mobile applications to disseminate the information. These mobile applications share parts of these information and help the passengers.

This project is an attempt to provide an accessible form of information dissemination instead of the face-to-face enquiry. The aim is to accommodate the passenger’s question with ease. For this Google Assistant is choosen in order to provide the enquiry in the form of conversation.
